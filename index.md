# Abba — Prayer & Quiet Time

Abba is an AI-powered Christian prayer companion. Speak your prayer, receive gentle scriptural reflection, and grow a steady Quiet Time rhythm — in 35 languages, designed to be simple enough for everyone, including seniors.

- Anonymous-first: start praying without making an account.
- Your audio, your data — analyzed, never sold, never used for AI training.
- Available on iOS (App Store).
